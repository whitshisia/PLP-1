About Me

I'm Whitney Shisia, a Communication and Media Studies student with a strong foundation in storytelling, digital media, and public engagement. My academic background has sharpened my skills in effective communication, creative thinking, and audience-centered content creation—elements I bring into every project I work on.

Beyond my media training, I specialize in backend development, where I focus on building secure, scalable, and efficient systems that drive the functionality of web and mobile applications. I have experience working with technologies like Python, Firebase, Node.js, and Express, and I'm proficient in building APIs, user authentication systems, real-time data features, and cloud integrations. I prioritize clean architecture, database optimization, and user privacy in all my backend work.

As a developer with a communications background, I bring a rare blend of technical execution and user-centric thinking to the table. I believe that code should not only work well but also serve people meaningfully. I'm continuously learning—currently exploring containerization (Docker), NoSQL databases, and scalable server deployments. Whether I'm contributing to a development team or working independently, I aim to build tools that empower users, enhance access, and spark innovation.
